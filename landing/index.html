Everything in one place

Replace the scattered mess with one simple system.

Families shouldn't need five logins to stay involved. Saphron Sua brings the essentials together with a calm, friendly interface.

Messaging

Conversations that feel modern — and stay safe.

Parents message teachers and admins directly. Teachers broadcast to their class. Students can only message their teacher, and every message is reviewed first.

  • Parent ↔ teacher and parent ↔ admin direct messages
  • Teacher → class announcements
  • Clean, chat-style threads
Safety first

Every student message is reviewed before it's delivered.

An automatic filter flags concerning language, and a teacher approves or rejects each message in a simple queue. Nothing reaches a recipient until a human says so.

  • Auto-screening for profanity, bullying and personal info
  • Teacher review queue
  • Parents can review their own child's activity
Events & tickets

Never miss a field trip — or a fix.

A shared calendar with one-tap RSVPs keeps families in the loop, while a built-in ticketing system routes IT, facilities and general requests to the right people.

  • Monthly calendar with RSVP
  • Submit & track tickets end-to-end
  • Admin assignment, priority and history
Rewards

Celebrate the good stuff.

Teachers award badges for kindness, effort and progress. Students see their achievements in a bright, encouraging board that makes school feel rewarding.

  • Visual badges & star points
  • Teacher-awarded, parent-visible
  • Documents & surveys built in too
